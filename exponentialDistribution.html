Exponential Distribution

Statistics

For a random variable x, with rate parameter "λ", the exponential distribution formula is given by:

f(x) = λ * exp(-λx) for x ≥ 0

Example: This distribution is often used to model the time between events, such as the lifespan of a product or the time until an event occurs (e.g., waiting time).
